This is a multiplayer tetris game toy project
Play as a normal tetris game, but beware, everytime a player clear a line, another random player will receive a random handicap for a while.
Wait room owner to start the game...
CONTROLS
LEFT move the piece left by 1
RIGHT move the piece right by 1
DOWN move the piece down by 1
SPACE drop the piece down
UP rotate the piece
SWIPE LEFT move the piece left by 1
SWIPE RIGHT move the piece right by 1
SWIPE DOWN move the piece down by 1
SWIPE UP drop the piece down
TAP rotate the piece
